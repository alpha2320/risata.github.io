By Muskan Ahuja

What is Risata?
Risata is a long-lived childhood dream come true. Pastry chef Muskan Ahuja, founder and head chef at Risata, realised early in life that desserts are her true calling, and she wanted to share this passion with the rest of the world. Unfortunately, genuine and authentic desserts are not easily available, and often they lack that personalized touch, and Risata is a humble attempt to change that. Our one true passion lies in creating the perfect dessert for our customers, using only high quality ingredients, utmost precision and of course a lot of love.
